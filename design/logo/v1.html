launch animation
The curve draws itself on app open

Splash sequence (looping here so you can watch it): wordmark fades in → hollow start pin appears → curve draws from left to right, rising → terracotta destination lands with a small bounce. Total ~1.6s, then it hands off to the auth or home screen.

Animation timing · wordmark fades in 0.0–0.6s · hollow start pin pops 0.4–0.8s · curve draws 0.75–2.3s · terracotta destination bounces 2.0–2.6s · holds, then fade to next screen. In SwiftUI, this is straightforward: trimmed on a Shape for the curve draw, .scaleEffect + .spring for the bounce, and .opacity with .animation for the fades.
