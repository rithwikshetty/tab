why this direction

Generic app design has converged on the same template — soft white cards, Inter, a purple gradient, a friendly rounded corner. tab is for one private friend group and one small set of human moments: we ate well, who paid, who owes whom by the end. A printed-ledger language fits that better than the standard template. It also keeps the logo's rising arc story intact — paper, ink, terracotta destination.

rules of the system

  • Numbers are mono. Always tabular, always right-set, currency code small and grey.
  • Names are serif. Italic Fraunces for trip names, descriptions, people in form sentences.
  • Labels are caps mono. All eyebrows, statuses, units — wide-tracked, never sentence case.
  • Dashed perforations separate transactional zones — never decorative.
  • Sage = owed to you. Terra = you owe. No other meaning for either color, ever.
  • The wax seal only appears the moment something becomes recorded — not on hover, not on drafts.
